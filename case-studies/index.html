Financial Systems Overhaul

A hospitality group managing multiple business entities

Challenge

Manual accounting across three businesses with no reconciliation process. Hundreds of bank transactions sat unprocessed. There was no profit and loss visibility and no way to understand the financial health of any entity at a glance.

Solution

We built a complete financial management system from scratch: automated ledger, line item categorisation, bank transaction matching, and reconciliation dashboards. Each entity received its own structured chart of accounts with unified reporting across the group.

Results

433 bank transactions processed and matched. Full profit and loss visibility across all entities. Monthly close reduced from days to hours. The finance team now spends time on analysis, not data entry.

Digital Infrastructure Cleanup

A multi-brand organisation with years of digital clutter

Challenge

Sixteen cloud drives with no structure. Years of accumulation had left critical documents buried under duplicates, outdated versions, and orphaned files. Sixteen gigabytes of duplicated content. No naming conventions, no ownership mapping, no way to find anything quickly.

Solution

We performed a complete audit of every drive and every file. Then we designed folder taxonomies, implemented naming conventions, mapped ownership per drive, and systematically reorganised the entire digital infrastructure.

Results

27,715 files organised. 16GB of duplicates identified and archived. Zero loose files remaining. Any document now findable in seconds. Clear ownership and structure that scales as the organisation grows.

Workflow Automation at Scale

A growing product company with manual processes

Challenge

Over twenty manual CRM processes eating into the team's day. Disconnected tools meant information lived in silos. Hours were lost weekly on repetitive tasks that should have been automatic: follow-ups, status updates, reporting, and data entry across systems.

Solution

We designed and deployed 76 automated workflows connecting the CRM, email, accounting, and project management systems into a single cohesive operation. Automated reporting and real-time alerts replaced manual check-ins.

Results

76 workflows running daily. 20 CRM automations handling lead management, follow-ups, and pipeline updates. Over 15 hours reclaimed every week. The team now focuses on selling and building, not on admin.

Hospitality Operations — Zero to Live

A villa services business launching from scratch

Challenge

A brand-new hospitality venture with nothing in place. No website, no booking system, no operational tools, no sales materials. The team needed to go from concept to revenue-ready as fast as possible.

Solution

We built the complete operations stack in under two weeks: a fully designed website, detailed service menus, dynamic pricing engine, booking flow, bilingual sales kit, QR-based guest experience system, and kitchen management tools. Everything the team needed to operate and sell from day one.

Results

Full website live and indexed. Booking system operational. Sales team equipped with professional materials in two languages. First client meeting booked within days of launch. The business went from zero to taking orders in under a fortnight.
